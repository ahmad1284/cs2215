What are lists?

In the html world we sometimes have to display lists

Lists are used to display a list of items

Lists can be ordered or unordered

Unordered list

        <ul>
          <li>This</li>
          <li>List</li>
          <li>Is</li>
          <li>Unordered</li>
        </ul>

Ordered list

        <ol>
          <li>This</li>
          <li>List</li>
          <li>Is</li>
          <li>Ordered</li>
        </ol>
